Pack Description

Originally a modified version of GregTech New Horizons, GT Impact is designed to be somewhat easier for players dissatisfied with the former pack's required Exploration, Combat, and Magic. The Modpack is entirely playable on peaceful difficulty, and thus requires no combat. In addition, the magic mods have been removed, allowing for players to focus on their factory, and only their factory.

GT Impact also has no questbook, unlike New Horizons which features nearly 2,000 Quests via the Better Questing mod to guide players through progression. This makes the pack more suited for experienced GregTech players who can follow progression without a questbook.
